V3 argued Imprint is "fan CRM today, verification rail tomorrow." The data validates this with comparable scale numbers.

| Layer | V4 sizing |
| --- | --- |
| **Wedge (artists at $15/mo)** | If 5,000 artists by year 2: ≈$900K ARR |
| **Lane 2: superfan economy** | Goldman TAM $4.5B, growing 21.5%/year |
| **Lane 3: fraud detection upstream** | $2-3B annual fraud, finite-pool model |
| **Lane 4: AI royalty defense** | €8.5B at risk per CISAC |
| **Total addressable, year 5** | $10B+ across superfan + fraud + AI defense |

The wedge (artist CRM) is real revenue today. The rail (verification protocol) is 100x TAM upside. Same product, two pricing models, two GTMs.
